1964 CDC 6600

Click here to download cdc6600.zip, a zip archive that contains reference manual, sales brochure, sales slide presentation and pictures of the 6600.
    http://www.textfiles.com/bitsavers/pdf/cdc/6x00/ contains a large archive of documents about the CDC 6600 family. I particularly recommend these:
  • Ralph Grishman's textbook on programming the 6600,
  • James Thornton's whitepaper Considerations in Computer Design and
  • Thornton's book Design of a Computer - The Control Data 6600.
